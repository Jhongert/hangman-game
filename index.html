    How to play

    Press play or any key to start the game. Once the game starts, the computer chooses a random word that you have to guess it by suggesting letters. The word to guess is represented by a row of underscores, representing each letter of the word.

    To guess a letter you can click the button that represnt the letter or you can press the key on your keyboard.

    If you suggest a letter which occurs in the word, the letter appears in all its correct positions and the button that represent that letter change to green. If the suggested letter does not occur in the word, you loss a live and the button turn red.

    When you loss all the lives, the game is over and you can try again.
